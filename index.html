ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Our Mission & Values

Soroptimist Marrakech is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering girls from rural areas through free secondary education. Our website is designed to focus on fundraising and awareness, reflecting our strong commitment to our mission and values that prioritize education and empowerment of young girls.

Our Mission

Empower girls from rural areas by providing them with free secondary education.
